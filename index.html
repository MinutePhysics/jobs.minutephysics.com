Writer / Editor / Coordinator for MinuteEarth
(YouTube Science Video Series)

MinuteEarth is a new video series in a style similar to MinutePhysics and its goal is to bring a fun perspective on earth, climate, and ecosystem science to the public!

We are currently developing the series and are seeking to bring one or two people on board to help run the show. We are looking for someone who is a great writer, has a passion for science outreach and education, enjoys collaborative projects, and can take initiative on researching/writing videos.
